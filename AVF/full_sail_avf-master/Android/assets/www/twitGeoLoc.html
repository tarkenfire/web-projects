In this demonstration, we will be combining the native feature of geolocation (via GPS, wifi triangulation, cell tower triangulation, etc) and a web service (in this case, twitter).

Press the button below to find tweets (with proper locations attached) within 10 miles of the current location of this device. If the location features of the device are not available (either due to being disabled or unavailable), an error will appear.
