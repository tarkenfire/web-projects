Index

Press one of the three buttons below to (potentially) showcase three types of notifications. All devices will support the first (text) notification. Devices with vibration motors (iPhone, Droid 4, etc) will be able to use the second (vibrate) notifications, but devices without (iPad, Nexus 7, etc), will not.

The third type of notification is audio, which is natively supported in Android, but not in iOS. Phonegap hacks around this by simply using a media player to play a sound file instead.
